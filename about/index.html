About

I have been working with web technologies for ten years, and have been involved in all aspects of the software development lifecycle - sales, design, development, production, project management, team management, and product retirement.

What interests me most about current technolgy is its abilty to automate away drudgery, discover new knowledge from large bodies of data, and connect people together to innovate and improve.

It strikes me that the internet itself is the basis upon which humainity becomes self-aware as a whole, adn it is this idea which drives me to participate in a movement towards something greater than the status quo.

The challenge I see, however, is how to take all this benefit without risking the livelyhood of greate swathes of humanity: technologiy’s great benefits, given at ever reduced costs, must be balenced against the need to provide people with a meaningful role in a new society.

And it is this challenge that I most enjoy engageing with in my career.

I live in Bristol, and enjoy yoga, woodwork and reading.
