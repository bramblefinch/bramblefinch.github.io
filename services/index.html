Services

I help make things better. This can be your product, the service you offer, or the way you go about doing what you do.

This improvement will likely mean changing the way you work, the way you communicate as a team, or even the technology you use.

How do I do this?

I encourage the use of open-source technology, and show people how it can improve both the quality of their work and the efficiency of how they go about it. I also show how you can gain further benefit by ‘passing on the payment’ and contributing to open source projects. I can then help recover the cost of this.

I demonstrate how adopting an Agile methodology can be used to manage risk of wasted effort, develop a more efficient team or workflow, and ultimately make you better at what you do.

Who do I work with to get this done?

I work at any level within an organisation.

For decision makers, this means education about modern software development and use so they can quickly make better, more informed decisions.

For a team of developers, designers, project managers and salespeople, I implement ways to use the software development process as a de-facto tool for better, clearer, and more risk-free communication. This brings immediate benefit to the product or service under offer, and with it a greater internal organisational efficiency.
